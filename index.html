Strategic Capital

Strategic Capital Across the Full Stack

Eastbridge Capital structures tailored funding solutions for property investment and development — from acquisition through to completion.

01

Senior Debt

First-mortgage facilities for acquisition, pre-development, construction, and investment lending.

02

Stretched Senior Debt

Higher-leverage senior structures that extend beyond traditional LVR thresholds while maintaining senior security.

03

Mezzanine Finance

Second mortgage or subordinated debt used to reduce equity requirements, bridge funding gaps, and optimise project feasibility.

04

Preferred Equity

Structured equity capital positioned between debt and common equity, providing funding while preserving sponsor control.

Every transaction is bespoke — structured to align risk, return, and timing for all parties.
