Our Process

How We Engage

From First Conversation to Final Settlement

Every client engagement at Eastbridge Capital follows a disciplined four-stage process — designed to ensure that the right capital is sourced, structured, and delivered efficiently with minimum disruption to your timeline.

Our process is not transactional. It is relational. We invest time upfront to understand your project and your objectives, so that every decision we make thereafter is aligned to your outcomes.

01

Step One

Strategic Assessment

Understanding the asset, the sponsor, and the funding objective. Before a solution can be designed, we need to understand your project intimately — its merits, its risks, and the capital outcome you require.

This stage typically involves a structured briefing, site review where appropriate, and review of feasibility, sales evidence, and project documentation.

02

Step Two

Structure & Positioning

Designing the optimal capital structure for your project. Once we understand the project and its requirements, we design a capital structure — determining the appropriate blend of senior debt, stretched senior, mezzanine, or equity.

We present a clear funding recommendation with indicative terms, pricing, and a shortlist of most suitable capital providers before any lender engagement commences.

03

Step Three

Targeted Capital Engagement

Engaging the most suitable funding partners with precision. Eastbridge Capital maintains active relationships with bank lenders, non-bank lenders, mezzanine funds, and private equity capital providers across Australia.

We present your transaction selectively — to the lenders most likely to approve it on the terms you require, protecting your project's confidentiality throughout the process.

04

Step Four

Execution & Close

Managing credit, documentation, and settlement end-to-end. We don't step back once a term sheet is issued. Eastbridge Capital manages the credit and documentation process actively — coordinating between borrower, lender, solicitors, and valuers to ensure smooth, timely settlement.

Post-settlement, we remain your ongoing capital partner — monitoring project progress and managing any restructures or extensions as required.

Typical Engagement

What to Expect

From initial briefing to unconditional credit approval, most transactions are completed within 10–15 business days.

D1

Initial Briefing — Day 1–2

We meet with you (in person or virtually) to understand the project, the capital requirement, and your timeline.

  • Project briefing and document collection
  • Preliminary feasibility review
  • Initial market sounding
D3

Structure Recommendation — Day 3–4

We present our recommended capital structure with indicative terms and preferred lender shortlist.

  • Capital structure recommendation
  • Indicative term sheet
  • Lender shortlist and rationale
D5

Lender Submission — Day 5–7

Credit applications are submitted to selected lenders simultaneously. Our submissions are comprehensive and pre-credit-approved in structure.

  • Formal credit submission to selected lenders
  • Valuation instruction coordination
  • Active management of lender queries
D10

Credit Approval & Documentation — Day 10–15

Formal credit approval received and loan documentation negotiated. Settlement coordinated with all parties.

  • Formal credit approval
  • Loan documentation review and negotiation
  • Settlement coordination and funding
